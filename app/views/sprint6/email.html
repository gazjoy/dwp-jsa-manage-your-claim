What happens next

We’ll check to make sure you’re eligible for New Style JSA.

If you’re eligible

If we decide you’re eligible, we’ll contact you about an appointment to agree your claimant commitment, usually within 10 working days. The appointment will normally be over the phone.

You’ll usually get your first payment within 5 working days of your appointment. We cannot pay you unless you attend the appointment.

Support during the appointment

If you need support during the appointment, you can have another person with you.

If you need a foreign language interpreter, you’ll need someone with you who can help with interpretation. We do not normally provide interpretation services.

Contact Jobcentre Plus before your appointment if you have a disability or health condition, for example a hearing impairment, which means you cannot attend a phone interview.

Change of circumstances

You must contact Jobcentre Plus to report a change of circumstances, such as starting a new job.

If you’re not eligible

If we decide you’re not eligible for New Style JSA, we’ll send you a letter explaining our decision, usually within 10 working days.
