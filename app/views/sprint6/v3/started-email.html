Dear ((first name))

We’ve started to process your New Style JSA application. If we have any questions about the details you’ve provided, we’ll let you know.

We’re getting a high number of applications due to coronavirus. We’re working hard to process your application as soon as we can, and we’ll usually contact you within 7 days to let you know if you can get New Style JSA.

If you’re eligible

If you can get New Style JSA, we’ll contact you about an appointment to agree what you need to do to look for work. This is called a ‘Claimant Commitment’ appointment and will normally be over the phone with a work coach from your local jobcentre.

When we call you, our phone number may show as unknown or start with 0800. When you answer, you’ll be able to ask questions to check the call is genuine.

We’ll aim to pay you within 7 days of your appointment.

If you’re not eligible

If you cannot get New Style JSA, we’ll send you a letter to explain why.

Do you need help with housing costs or support for a partner or child?

If you’re on a low income you might be able to apply for Universal Credit as part of a dual claim with New Style JSA.

Unlike New Style JSA, you can apply with a partner and get support for housing costs, children and childcare.

You or your partner must have less than £16,000 in savings or investments to be eligible.

Find out how to claim Universal Credit on GOV.UK.

Next steps

While we process your application, you should:

  • make a record of the jobsites you’ve registered on
  • make sure you’ve uploaded your latest CV to jobsites
  • let us know if your circumstances change, for example if you get a job
