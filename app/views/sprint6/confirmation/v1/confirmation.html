Application complete

We’ll send you confirmation of your application by text and email.

What happens next

We’ll check to make sure you’re eligible for New Style JSA.

If you’re eligible

If we decide you’re eligible, we’ll contact you about an appointment to agree your claimant commitment, usually within 10 working days. The appointment will normally be over the phone.

You’ll usually get your first payment within 5 working days of your appointment.

If you’re not eligible

If we decide you’re not eligible, we’ll send you a letter explaining our decision, usually within 10 working days.
