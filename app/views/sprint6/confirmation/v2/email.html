What happens next

We’ll check to make sure you’re eligible for New Style JSA.

If you’re eligible

If we decide you’re eligible, we’ll contact you about an appointment to agree the kinds of things you should do to prepare and look for work. This is called a claimant commitment. We’ll usually contact you about the appointment within 14 days. The appointment will normally be with a work coach over the phone.

We’ll aim to pay you within 7 days of your appointment. We cannot pay you unless you attend this appointment.

Support during the appointment

If you need support during the appointment, you can have another person with you.

If you need a foreign language interpreter, you’ll need someone with you who can help with interpretation. We do not normally provide interpretation services.

Contact Jobcentre Plus before your appointment if you have a disability or health condition, for example a hearing impairment, which means you cannot attend a phone interview.

Jobcentre Plus

  • Telephone: 0800 055 6688
  • Textphone: 0800 023 4888
  • Relay UK (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0800 055 6688
  • Video relay service for British Sign Language (BSL) users - check you can use the service
  • Welsh language: 0800 012 1888
  • Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm
  • Find out about call charges

If you’re not eligible

If we decide you’re not eligible, we’ll send you a letter explaining our decision. We’ll usually send the letter within 14 days.

Change of circumstances

You must contact Jobcentre Plus to report a change of circumstances, such as starting a new job.

If we do not contact you

Contact Jobcentre Plus if we do not contact you within xx working days of submitting your application.

Accessibility

If you have a disability or health condition, we can provide you with information in a different format, for example:

  • Braille
  • large print
  • audio

More information is available on GOV.UK.
